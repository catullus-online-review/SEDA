The project

Welcome to Catullus Online Review .

This website was created to host the review of the SDE, Catullus Online, published in 2013 by Dàniel Kiss.

This review was created as a final exam project for the course, SEDA (Scholarly Editing and Digital Approaches), held by Paola Maria Carmela Italia.

This review was awarded the third place in the SEDA prize competition, in the year 2021/2022
